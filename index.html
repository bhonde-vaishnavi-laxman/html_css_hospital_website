    About Saideep Hospital

    Welcome To Saideep Hospital

    “The starting point of all achievement is desire”


    – Napoleon Hill




    Saideep was initially conceptualized by Dr.Deepak S.S in early 2012 as a family project. The idea attracted other esteemed colleagues and they joined in. Soon enough they were on a blueprint towards inception and before they knew, the dream molded into its current glory, and they have been successfully delivering through all nodes of the medical fraternity.
    The uniqueness of the project is that all the consultants have moved into Saideep with their former credibility and infrastructural resources after closing their existing ones.
    The desire to stay visionary in leading the healthcare domain and dedicating the journey towards transforming the lives of people through excellent clinical quality is what keeps Saideep on its feet and drives it through!
